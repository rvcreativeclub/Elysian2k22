About Us

Ravi Varma Creative Club

Ravi Varma Creative Club is one of Kongu Engineering College's emerging clubs. All of your remarkable and one-of-a-kind abilities can be showcased in one spot. This is a new club in KEC looking for young talents who want to develop their inner potential. Prepare to experience a club that is not only a venue to showcase your talent, but also a sanctuary of artistry and inspiration where your imagination is the only restriction!
